Bangladesh 2.0

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Drives innovation through digital transformation, AI, and fintech. It aims for a smart, sustainable, and globally competitive future.

Sustainability

Prioritizes sustainability with green energy, smart infrastructure, and climate resilience. It aims for an eco-friendly and future-ready economy.

Infrastructure

Focuses on modern infrastructure with smart cities, digital connectivity, and sustainable transport. It aims to drive economic growth and urban transformation.

Empowerment

Empowers people through digital inclusion, education, and entrepreneurship. It fosters innovation, equality, and economic growth.
