Bangladesh 2.0

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Drives innovation through digital transformation, AI, and fintech. It aims for a smart, sustainable, and globally competitive future.

Sustainability

Prioritizes sustainability with green energy, smart infrastructure, and climate resilience. It aims for an eco-friendly and future-ready economy.

Infrastructure

Focuses on modern infrastructure with smart cities, digital connectivity, and sustainable transport. It aims to drive economic growth and urban transformation.

Empowerment

Empowers people through digital inclusion, education, and entrepreneurship. It fosters innovation, equality, and economic growth.

The Vision of Future

Envisions a smart, sustainable, and tech-driven nation with a strong digital economy, green energy, and inclusive growth. It aims for innovation-led development, global competitiveness, and enhanced quality of life for all citizens.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation focuses on expanding internet access, digital services, and e-governance to empower citizens and businesses. It aims to build a tech-savvy workforce, boost innovation, and foster a digital economy for inclusive growth.

Green Energy

Aims to transition to green energy by investing in renewable sources like solar, wind, and biomass to reduce carbon footprints. The focus is on sustainable development, energy security, and addressing climate change challenges for a greener future.

Modern Infrastructure

Envisions modern infrastructure with smart cities, advanced transportation systems, and sustainable urban planning. This includes upgrading roads, ports, and utilities to support economic growth and improve the quality of life for citizens.
